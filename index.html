All India Tourist Permit

Our vehicles hold valid All India Tourist Permits — legally operating across every state and union territory of India.

🛡️ What is All India Permit?

  • Legal permission to run taxi service across all Indian states
  • Issued by Punjab Regional Transport Authority (RTA)
  • Valid for interstate travel — no border checkpoint issues
  • Mandatory for long-distance tourist cab services
  • Covers all 28 States + 8 Union Territories

✅ Your Benefit as Passenger

  • No police trouble at any state border
  • Full legal protection for you and driver
  • Insured vehicle travel across all of India
  • Professional, verified, experienced drivers only
  • Complete peace of mind on long journeys

📋 Our Permit Details

  • Type: All India Tourist Vehicle Permit
  • Authority: Punjab Regional Transport Office
  • Base Office: Fazilka, Punjab, India
  • Coverage: Nationwide — all states & UTs
  • Papers regularly renewed & always valid
